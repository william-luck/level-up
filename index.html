Level Up Progress Tracker

What do I do?

Complete tasks to level up your skills. Each task corresponds to one of four skills:

  • Self compassion
  • Bravery
  • Self care
  • Willingness to ask for help

As you complete tasks, the progress bars will fill up.
Once a skill reaches 100%, you will earn a reward, which will be equipped by your avatar.

As you complete tasks, you have the option to add a comment for future reference, to give yourself credit where credit is due.
You can complete up to 20 tasks (five per skill), so you have the option to hide your task log for ease of use.
